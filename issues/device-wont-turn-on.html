Issue Guide

Device will not turn on

If your BOOX shows no response at all, start with charging and button timing before assuming the battery or screen has failed.

No response Power Startup

Common signs

  • The screen stays blank even after pressing the power button.
  • No charging light or vibration appears.
  • The device worked recently but now seems completely dead.
  • The problem started after the battery drained fully.

Possible causes

  • The battery is deeply discharged and needs more charging time.
  • The charger, cable, or power outlet is not delivering power.
  • The device is frozen and needs a long power-button hold.
  • There may be a charging-port, battery, or board-level hardware issue.

Step-by-step troubleshooting

  1. Charge the device for at least 30 to 60 minutes. Use a wall charger and a cable you trust. A fully drained BOOX can take time before it shows signs of life.
  2. Check the cable, charger, and outlet. Test another cable or adapter if possible so you do not mistake a charger problem for a device problem.
  3. Hold the power button longer than usual. Try a steady hold for 10 to 15 seconds, then wait a full minute to see whether the device begins to start.
  4. Look closely for faint screen changes. E Ink screens can appear blank even when power is present. Watch for a logo flash, battery icon, or refresh effect.
  5. Disconnect accessories. Remove cases with magnets, USB accessories, and memory cards before trying again.
  6. Stop after one or two proper attempts. If there is still no response after charging and a controlled long press, the safest next step is service or official BOOX support.

Important warning

Stop immediately if the device gets unusually hot, smells burnt, or the battery appears swollen. Do not keep charging a device that shows those signs.

When to contact support

Contact BOOX support if the device stays completely unresponsive after at least 30 to 60 minutes of charging with known-good accessories and a proper long power-button hold.

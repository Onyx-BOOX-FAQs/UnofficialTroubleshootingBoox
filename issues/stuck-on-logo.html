Issue Guide

Device stuck on BOOX logo

Use this page when the device powers on and shows the BOOX logo, but never finishes loading into the home screen.

Startup issue Boot problem Reset caution

Common signs

  • The BOOX logo stays on screen for several minutes without changing.
  • The device restarts and returns to the logo again.
  • The screen has power, but the home screen never loads.
  • The issue started after charging, an update, or a forced shutdown.

Possible causes

  • The device battery is too low to complete startup.
  • A recent update or app change interrupted the boot process.
  • The system needs a long restart after a temporary freeze.
  • Internal storage or firmware may need recovery support.

Step-by-step troubleshooting

  1. Leave the device charging for at least 30 minutes. Use a known-good cable and wall charger. A BOOX device with a very low battery may show the logo before it has enough power to finish booting.
  2. Perform a long press on the power button. Hold the power button for about 10 to 15 seconds, then wait another full minute to see whether the system completes startup on its own.
  3. Try one controlled restart, not repeated rapid restarts. If the device responds, allow it to shut down fully before turning it back on. Repeating forced restarts too quickly can make diagnosis harder.
  4. Disconnect accessories and remove external variables. Unplug USB accessories, memory cards, or hubs if any are connected, then test startup again.
  5. Think about what changed before the problem began. If the issue started after an update, app install, or battery drain event, note that detail because it helps determine whether software recovery is more likely.
  6. Stop before factory reset unless simpler checks have failed. A reset may erase user data. If the device still will not pass the logo screen after charging and one proper restart attempt, support or a product-specific recovery method is the safer next step.

What to note before support

Write down whether the problem started after an update, a full battery drain, or a forced shutdown. That detail helps support narrow the cause faster.

Important warning

Do not press hard on the E Ink screen, repeatedly force power cycles, or factory reset the device unless you understand the data risk. If there are signs of swelling, impact damage, or water exposure, stop troubleshooting and seek service.

When to contact support

Contact BOOX support if the device remains on the logo screen after charging, a controlled restart, and basic accessory removal. Include the model name, software version if known, and what happened right before the issue started.

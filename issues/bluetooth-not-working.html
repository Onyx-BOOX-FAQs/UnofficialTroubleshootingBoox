Issue Guide

Bluetooth not working

Use this guide for pairing failures, missing accessories, or Bluetooth connections that keep dropping.

Bluetooth Pairing Accessory

Common signs

  • The accessory does not appear in the Bluetooth list.
  • Pairing starts but never finishes.
  • The connection drops after a short time.
  • The accessory works with other devices but not with BOOX.

Possible causes

  • The accessory is not in pairing mode.
  • An old saved pairing is causing conflicts.
  • The accessory may not be fully compatible with the BOOX model.
  • Bluetooth radio issues may need restart or support.

Step-by-step troubleshooting

  1. Confirm the accessory is in pairing mode. Many keyboards, styluses, and speakers have a limited pairing window.
  2. Forget old pairings and try again. Remove the accessory from the BOOX Bluetooth list if it already appears there.
  3. Restart both devices. A fresh restart often clears connection states that block pairing.
  4. Move the devices close together. Pairing is more reliable at short range with minimal interference.
  5. Test the accessory with another device. That confirms whether the problem is the accessory or the BOOX.
  6. Check for model-specific limits. Some accessories work only with certain apps or input modes.

Important warning

Do not assume a device fault if only one accessory fails. Compatibility differences are common with Bluetooth peripherals.

When to contact support

Contact BOOX support if Bluetooth cannot detect any accessories after restart, or if pairing used to work and now fails with multiple devices.

Issue Guide

Battery draining too fast

Use this guide if your BOOX loses charge much faster than usual during reading, standby, or note-taking.

Battery Drain Standby

Common signs

  • The battery drops quickly even during light use.
  • Standby time is much shorter than before.
  • The device runs warmer than usual.
  • The drain started after an update, app install, or sync change.

Possible causes

  • Wi-Fi, Bluetooth, syncing, or app activity is staying active in the background.
  • Brightness or refresh settings are higher than necessary.
  • A recent update or app change increased power usage.
  • The battery may be aging or damaged.

Step-by-step troubleshooting

  1. Reduce brightness and disable unused radios. Turn off Wi-Fi and Bluetooth when you do not need them.
  2. Restart the device. This can stop background tasks that keep draining power.
  3. Think about recent changes. If the problem started after a new app or update, test again after removing that variable.
  4. Check standby behavior overnight. A large drain while idle points to a background-process issue rather than screen usage.
  5. Watch for heat. Warmth during light use often means the device is doing more work than expected.
  6. Compare against your normal use. Heavy note-taking, Wi-Fi syncing, or audio can drain faster than plain reading.
  7. Check your settings Go to settings and click the "battery", make sure that the Battery Manager is on to reduce background processes.

Important warning

If the battery drains quickly and the device also swells, overheats, or shuts down suddenly, stop using it and seek service.

When to contact support

Contact BOOX support if the battery drain remains severe after restart and basic power-saving changes, or if the device gets unusually warm during light use.

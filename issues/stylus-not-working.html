Issue Guide

Stylus not working

Use this guide if the pen does not write, misses strokes, or only works in certain apps or areas of the screen.

Stylus Writing Input

Common signs

  • The pen stops writing completely.
  • Lines appear broken or delayed.
  • The stylus works in one app but not another.
  • Input fails more often near one edge of the display.

Possible causes

  • The nib is worn, loose, or not seated correctly.
  • The app or tool currently selected does not support pen input as expected.
  • The stylus may not be fully compatible with the BOOX model.
  • The screen or digitizer may be damaged.

Step-by-step troubleshooting

  1. Check the nib first. Replace or reseat it if it looks worn or uneven.
  2. Test in another app. If the stylus works in notes but not in one app, the issue may be software-specific.
  3. Restart the device. A restart can clear temporary input glitches.
  4. Remove cases or accessories that may interfere. Strong magnets or thick covers can affect input on some devices.
  5. Test different screen areas. If only one area fails, the problem may be hardware rather than the stylus itself.
  6. Verify compatibility. Not every EMR pen behaves the same across all BOOX models.

Important warning

Do not scratch or press hard on the display while testing the stylus. Use light, normal writing pressure only.

When to contact support

Contact BOOX support if the stylus fails in multiple apps, only part of the screen accepts input, or the problem started after physical impact.

Issue Guide

Forgot password or locked out

Use this guide if you cannot unlock the device and need to understand the safest recovery options before trying a reset.

Lock screen Password Data risk

Common signs

  • The password or PIN is no longer accepted.
  • You cannot remember the current unlock method.
  • The device is usable physically but blocked by the lock screen.
  • You are considering a factory reset to regain access.

Possible causes

  • The password was changed and not recorded.
  • The device is using a different unlock method than expected.
  • The account recovery path may be limited on that model.
  • A reset may be the only remaining option, depending on the setup.

Step-by-step troubleshooting

  1. Pause before repeated attempts. Too many failed entries can complicate access or increase delay timers.
  2. Check for the exact unlock method. Confirm whether the device expects a PIN, password, pattern, or account credential.
  3. Look for any official recovery option tied to your account. Use only BOOX-supported methods for your model.
  4. Decide whether local data must be preserved. That changes whether a reset is acceptable.
  5. Do not reset unless you understand the consequences. A factory reset can erase local notes, downloads, and unsynced files.
  6. Move to official support if you are unsure. Lock recovery is one of the cases where model-specific guidance matters most.

Important warning

A factory reset can erase local files. If your notes or documents were never synced or backed up, stop and look for an official recovery path first.

When to contact support

Contact BOOX support if you cannot confirm the correct unlock method, need model-specific recovery instructions, or want to avoid unnecessary data loss.

Before you start troubleshooting

  • Use a known-good cable and charger before assuming the device is dead.
  • Give BOOX devices extra time after a full battery drain or large update.
  • Do not press hard on the E Ink display while testing the screen.
  • Write down the model name and recent changes if you may need support later.

When to stop and get help

  • Stop if the battery looks swollen, the device gets unusually hot, or you smell burning.
  • Stop if there is liquid damage, major impact damage, or a cracked screen panel.
  • Be careful with factory resets because they can erase local data.
  • Use BOOX support when a guide says the next step may need service or model-specific recovery.
